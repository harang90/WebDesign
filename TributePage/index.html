Roh Moo-hyun

The Ninth President of the Republic of Korea

In office
25 February 2003 - 24 February 2008

Timeline

  • 1946 - Born into a poor farming family in Bongha village, Gimhae
  • 1975 - Passed the Korean National Bar Exam
  • 1981 - Defended students who had been tortured for suspicion of possession of contraband literature
  • 1985 - Started to participate in civic movements by assuming permanent power of attorney on behalf of the Busan council of citizen democracy
  • 1988 - Elected as a member of the National Assembly, representing Dong-gu, Busan
  • 2000 - Appointed Minister of Maritime Affairs and Fisheries under Kim Dae-jung presidency
  • 2002 - Won the presidency on 19 December 2002, by defeating Lee Hoi-chang with a narrow 2% margin of victory

"After that defense, my life was totally changed. At first, even I coundn't believe that they had been tortured that harshly. However, when I saw their horrified eyes and their missing toenails, my comfortable life as a lawyer came to an end."

President Roh, after defending students against the National Security Act
